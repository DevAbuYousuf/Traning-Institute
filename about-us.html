About Us

Welcome To Daffodil Technical Institute (DTI), The Best Polytechnic Institute

Daffodil Technical Institute (DTI), recognized as the best polytechnic institute in Dhaka, offers a Diploma in Engineering affiliated with the Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB). DTI provides a comprehensive 4-year diploma program designed to equip students with the practical skills and theoretical knowledge required in various engineering fields. With state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a focus on hands-on training, DTI ensures that students are well-prepared for successful careers in engineering
